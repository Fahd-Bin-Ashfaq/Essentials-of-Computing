## ðŸ§© What is Prompt Engineering?

Prompt Engineering is the skill of **writing clear and effective instructions (prompts)** to get accurate and useful results from AI tools.

### Good vs Bad Prompt

* **Bad Prompt:** "Write about AI"
* **Good Prompt:** "Explain Artificial Intelligence for college students using simple language and real-life examples in 5 bullet points."

---

## ðŸ”— Prompt Chaining (Advanced Concept)

Breaking a **complex task into smaller prompts** to get better AI outputs.

**Example:**

1. Generate ideas for a presentation
2. Create slide-wise content
3. Improve language and tone
